A random musing about a brilliant yet grim video I recently watched.

_Unthinkable ~ Horses_ (YouTube)

This was quite a hard watch. Hard, but necessary. Necessary, since it's subject matter is reality. Though, is it _really_ necessary to know about everything? To dive into the deepest caverns about subjects? In this case, subjects that leaves any rational being searching for hope in humanity?

**Specialization of thought**: to absolve yourself from thinking about the larger systems at play and focusing on those conversations that you can impact. Focusing on those people you can influence and inspire. This counters philosophizing deeply on subject matters and, instead, viewing the subject matter as-is. Not letting its action or subsequent consequence affect you - just to see it from a first-principles perspective.

However, I do believe that having an educated stance on the subject matter, maybe on an inferential level, is a sustainable way to treat hard topics.

educated inference > philosophizing
