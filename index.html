about

I am a Master's student at Georgia Tech advised by Animesh Garg. I study how learned models behave and turn those insights into robust systems. My interest centers on reinforcement learning, world models (neural simulators), and gradient-based optimization.


I believe the key to building more intelligent systems is to expose them to diverse (often challenging) environments, study their dynamics in such environments, and use that understanding to design efficient, capable algorithms. This also mirrors how I approach my research.


If you are interested in working together or have any questions, feel free to reach out via email!
